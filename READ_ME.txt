

Simple Shooting Game
	You control a dog inide a back yard. Walk around
	and shot the 5 targets set up around the yard. 
	The gane will automatically quit, when target 
	goal is reached or when "y" is pressed.

Controls:

W: Move Forward
A: Turn Left
S: Move Backward
D: Turn Right
K: Shoot Projectile
O: Jump
Y: Quit Game
